Amenities

We have a terrace area where you can eat alfresco whilst enjoying the magnificent views. A peaceful shady garden to relax in, a hot tub for you to chill in and a splash pool to cool down in.

Food

At Vue de Vallon we take pride in providing our guests with the best regional produce. Fresh eggs from our own hens, bread and pastries from the artisan bakery in the next village, homemade jams, Limousin beef and local honey, sausages and even snails. Join us for breakfast every day, whilst additionally dinner, afternoon tea, picnics, barbecues and themed events are available to order.
