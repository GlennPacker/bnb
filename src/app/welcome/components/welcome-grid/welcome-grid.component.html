Vue de Vallon offers five light airy rooms in a peaceful village setting. The rooms are decorated in typical French rustic style with comfortable beds, fresh linen and en-suite or private bathrooms. We aim to make your stay as relaxing as possible. Situated in the Haute Vienne region of France (previously known as the Limousin), Vue de Vallon is surrounded by lakes, rivers and picturesque French villages. Whether cycling, kayaking, walking, crafting or exploring are your passion - there's something for everyone.
